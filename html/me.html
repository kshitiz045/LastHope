B. Tech (Mechanical Engineering)

It is an 8 semester Bachelor's degrees program in Mechanical Engineering after 10+2 or equivalent. Mechanical engineering is one of the oldest and broadest engineering disciplines that apply the principles of physics and materials science for analysis, design, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It is the branch of engineering that involves the production and usage of heat and mechanical power for the design, production, and operation of machines and tools. With the world evolving every second the mechanical engineering has grown its roots into other domains thereby giving rise to more contemporary courses/subject such as- robotics, automation and many others .
